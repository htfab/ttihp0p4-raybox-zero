## How it works

A pair of 8-bit inputs are summed with a regular hardware adder, and is presented as the output.

## How to test

Set `ui_in` to one 8-bit value, and `uio` to the other, and the sum should appear on `uo_out`.

## External hardware

No special external hardware needed. TT devkit is all you need!
